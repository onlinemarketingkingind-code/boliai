# 🌉 Inter-State Communication Use Cases

## The Domestic India Challenge

India has **45+ million inter-state migrants** who face daily language barriers. BoliAI's **Bharat Mode** addresses this critical need.

---

## 🎯 Target User Segments

### 1. 🏗️ **Migrant Workers (25M+ users)**

#### Profile
- Construction workers, factory laborers, domestic help
- Moving from rural areas to metros
- Limited formal education
- Need survival communication

#### Pain Points
- Can't read local signboards
- Struggle to buy groceries
- Fear of being cheated in markets
- Can't access emergency services
- Social isolation

#### BoliAI Solution
```
Survival Pack (Voice-First Interface):
✅ Market phrases with audio
✅ Emergency contacts with one-tap call
✅ Picture-based learning (no reading required)
✅ Offline mode for low connectivity areas
✅ Regional price guides to avoid cheating
```

**Example Journey:**
```
Tamil construction worker in Mumbai:
Day 1: "पानी कहाँ है?" (Where is water?)
Week 1: Market shopping phrases
Month 1: Workplace communication
Month 3: Making local friends
```
